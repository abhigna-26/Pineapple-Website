Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

At Pineapple, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or work with us.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us through our contact forms, email interactions, or service requests. This may include:

  • Your name and job title
  • Contact details including email address and phone number
  • Organization or business information
  • Project scopes, operations, and requirements

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect to understand your project needs and provide bespoke engineering and support services. Specifically for:

  • Internal record keeping and client onboarding
  • Communicating with you regarding project updates, scope, and technical proposals
  • Improving our service offerings and customer relations
  • Ensuring security of custom systems we develop for you

3. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your explicit permission or are required by law to do so.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at pineapple.apt26@gmail.com.
